Empowering Wardrobes, Since 1977

In the pursuit of making fashion accessible to all, Parshwa emerged in 1997 with a commitment to delivering economical and affordable gentlemen's garments. Over the years, our focus has been unwavering, specializing in crafting low and medium-range gentlemen's clothing across a spectrum of fabrics, including cotton, polyester, linen, rayon, and more.

Our Distinctive Offerings

At Parshwa, we pride ourselves on being a one-stop destination for dressing solutions that cater to everyone, from the little ones to the distinguished gentlemen. Our range spans from size 16 to 52 in shirts, ensuring a perfect fit for every individual. Additionally, our collection of gentlemen's trousers is available in sizes ranging from 28 to 42.

Industrial Uniform Solutions

We understand that every individual is unique, and their clothing should reflect that. Hence, at Parshwa, we go beyond ready-made garments. Our expertise extends to made-to-order services, ensuring that your clothing is not just an outfit but a statement of your distinct style.

For businesses seeking uniformity with a touch of professionalism, Parshwa is your reliable partner. We undertake industrial uniform orders, providing tailored solutions that blend functionality with a smart, cohesive appearance.

With an eye on global markets, we extend our services to include Free On Board (FOB) solutions. Whether you are seeking quality garments for your retail business or specific fashion needs, Parshwa ensures seamless transactions and delivery to your specified location.
